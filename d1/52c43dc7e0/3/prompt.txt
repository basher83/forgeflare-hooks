2. Pane creation — uses `cmux new-split` to open a right-side split pane and launches `loop.sh` (with any forwarded arguments) inside it.

3. Screen polling — every 15 seconds, it captures the last 200 lines of that pane via `cmux read-screen`, hashes the output, and reacts based on what it sees:
